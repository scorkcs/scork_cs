Our Expertise

  • Project Management: Comprehensive delivery of healthcare IT and turnkey software projects, from setting up datacenters and infrastructure to system implementation, go-live, and post-live support. Successfully managed EMR, PACS, RIS, VNA, and other healthcare technology projects across India, Singapore, Qatar, and the United States.
  • Healthcare Interoperability: HL7, FHIR, DICOM, interface engines (middleware), and data ingestion solutions enabling seamless connectivity across systems.
  • Integration & Compliance: Safe, effective, and regulatory-aligned solutions ensuring optimized workflows and sustainable outcomes.
  • Data Management & Migration: Expertise in healthcare data conversion, migration, and consolidation with a focus on accuracy, security, and compliance.
  • Product Consulting & Selection: Guiding healthcare organizations in evaluating and selecting the right solutions tailored to their clinical and operational needs.
  • Tender Management & Support: End-to-end support in preparing, reviewing, and managing healthcare tenders, ensuring competitive advantage and compliance.
